# nxtfix Car Maintenance Tracker
Many drivers forget when important car maintenance was last completed or when the next service is due. Missing services such as oil changes, tire rotations, and brake checks can lead to bigger mechanical problems and higher repair costs. nxtfix helps users keep vehicle and maintenance information organized in one place so they can stay on top of routine car care. The target users for nxtfix are everyday drivers and vehicle owners who want a simple way to track maintenance. The app is designed for people who may not know much about cars but still want to keep their vehicle in good condition and avoid missing important services. The user begins by registering for an account or logging in. After entering the app, the user adds vehicle information such as make, model, year, and current mileage. The user can then enter maintenance records, including the type of service, the date it was completed, and the mileage at the time of service. The app stores this information and compares the vehicle’s current mileage to the mileage of previous maintenance records. If the mileage difference shows that a service may be due soon, the app displays an alert. The user can also view maintenance history and check the dashboard for an organized summary of the vehicle’s records and service needs.
## Key Features of the Application
- User registration and login
- Add and store vehicle information
- Update current vehicle mileage
- Add maintenance records
- View maintenance history
- Check for due maintenance
- Show alerts when service may be due soon
- Display vehicle details in a simple dashboard
